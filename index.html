Visit Ilorin , Nigeria

Ilorin is the capital city of Kwara State located in the Western region of Nigeria. Although Ilorin is classified under the North-Central geopolitical zone, the city is considered a Yoruba city by all historical and sociological standards.

Top three activities to do at Ilorin

Owu Waterfalls, located near Ilorin, offers a breathtaking display of cascading waters surrounded by lush vegetation. Explore the nature trails, take a dip in the refreshing pools, and marvel at the beauty of this enchanting waterfall.

Situated by a scenic waterside, Waterview Gardens is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. It features nature trails, panoramic views, and a peaceful ambience, making it a great spot for hiking, picnics, and leisurely walks.
